Bicycle wheel simulator

An interactive app for designing and testing virtual bicycle wheels. Choose your hub dimensions, rim, and spoke number and type. Add forces like rider wight, pedaling torque, or lateral loads. Plot deformation and spoke tensions, and calculate properties like mass, stiffness, and strength.

About this website

bicyclewheel.info is a free resource for designing and understanding bicycle wheels. It is a side-project by Dr. Matt Ford, author of Reinventing the Wheel: Stress Analysis, Stablity, and Optimization of the Bicycle Wheel.
